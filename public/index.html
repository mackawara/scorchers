Coaching Philosophy

Our philosophy is guided by the thinking that children can learn sporting skills the same way they learn how to walk/chew i.e without anyone teaching them the technique. Coaching consists of creating a fun and safe environment where children are exposed to situations in which they have to come up with game plans and solutions
